§ 02About Grave 9

27 years of producing events that actually deliver.

Founded in 1998, Grave 9 Productions LLC is a full-service live event production company with decades of experience producing events of every scale throughout the United States.

From small venue productions and branded activations to multi-day festivals and nationally supported broadcast events, our team understands what it takes to execute memorable experiences under real-world conditions.

We specialize in partnering with broadcast companies, sponsors, promoters, venues, and brands to create events that not only run flawlessly behind the scenes, but deliver an unforgettable fan experience in front of the stage.

Our approach combines technical production expertise, operational planning, sponsor integration, crowd engagement, and on-site execution to ensure every event achieves its goals while exceeding audience expectations.

For more than 25 years, Grave 9 Productions has built a reputation for reliability, creativity, and professionalism in live entertainment production.
